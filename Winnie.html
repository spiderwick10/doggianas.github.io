Wintermelon 5161
Chihuahua Mix
Female
Small (6-20 lbs)
Status: In hospice care

Wintermelon is a sweet, shy Chihuahua who came to us after being found as a stray.

This darling girl needs an understanding, gentle adopter who is willing to take it slow and gain her trust. She is a nervous girl, but we know she will blossom more and more each day once she’s in a forever home. Wintermelon is quiet, sweet, and great with other dogs. She will be forever grateful to the person who gives her a chance!

Due to cognitive dysfunction, Wintermelon is available for adoption through Muttville’s hospice care program.
Wintermelon is 12 years young and weighs 7.5 pounds.

Hear about Wintermelon here!

After being diagnosed with a terminal illness, Wintermelon is living life to the fullest in our Hospice Program, under which one of our special hospice families has provided her with a loving home and the very best possible end-of-life care. Please wish Wintermelon well!
