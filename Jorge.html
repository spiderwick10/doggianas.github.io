Jorge 4785
Chihuahua Mix
Male
Small (6-20 lbs)
Status: In hospice care

Jorge is an adorable little Chihuahua who comes to us from San Jose.
This sweet boy lives to be held, and instantly begins to relax and fall asleep in your lap. He would make a wonderful companion for someone looking for a mellow and gentle best friend. Jorge is deaf and blind, but that doesn’t stop him from exploring his surroundings and being an outgoing little guy. He loves going for walks and smelling his way around parks. Jorge is also great with other dogs! We know Jorge’s future is bright…all he needs is a family to call his own!

Jorge is 12 years young and weighs 7 pounds.
After being diagnosed with a terminal illness, Jorge is living life to the fullest in our Hospice Program, under which one of our special hospice families has provided him with a loving home and the very best possible end-of-life care. Please wish Jorge well!
